Protein Pasta

Description of Dish

A protein-rich lunch for everyone to enjoy! Just like my protein waffle breakfast, this meal is something that I could eating every single day of the week! It's quite filling, obviously macro-friendly and most importantly super delicious! (Plus, pasta!)

Ingredients Needed:

  • Meat of your choice (I prefer chicken or sausage)
  • A pasta of your choice (I enjoy farfalle, penne, rotini and spaghetti)
  • 1/2 container of sauce (I use tomato basil)
  • Oregano/italian herb blend (adjust to taste as needed)
  • Garlic powder (adjust to taste as needed)
  • Cracked black pepper (adjust to taste as needed)
  • Optional: 1 tbsp red pepper flakes (adjust to taste as needed)

Steps:

  1. Bring 2-3 quarts of salted water to boil. Add pasta of choice and cook for 7-8 mins (or according to package)
  2. Prepare protein as needed.
    • - For chicken, I bake at 350Â°F for 12-13 mins per side.
    • - For sausage (pre-cooked), I slice in 1/4" rounds.
  3. Add pasta sauce of choice to saucepan. Heat on medium to medium-high heat until small, continuous bubbles start to form.
  4. Season sauce to personal preference.
  5. Add prepared protein to sauce and stir, ensuring everything is coated properly.
  6. Drain and rinse pasta w/ cold water, add to saucepan.
  7. Stir until pasta and protein are evenly coated in sauce.
  8. Add to plate or pasta bowl.
  9. Enjoy!
