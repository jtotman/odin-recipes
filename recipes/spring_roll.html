Spring Roll in a Bowl

Description of Dish

This is what I've been typically eating for dinners as of late. My mother introduced me to the original recipe, and I have since made my own variation!

Ingredients Needed:

  • 1b ground meat of your choice (I prefer chicken)
  • A slaw mix of your choice (I use broccoli slaw)
  • 1 cup rice of your choice (I use fragrant jasmine rice)
  • 4 tbsp low sodium soy sauce (adjust to taste as needed)
  • 1 tsp garlic powder (adjust to taste as needed)
  • 1 tbsp ground ginger (adjust to taste as needed)
  • Optional: 1 tbsp red pepper flakes (adjust to taste as needed)

Steps:

  1. Bring 2 cups of water to boil. Salt, add 1 cup rice then let simmer on low heat for 15 minutes.
    • - Remove from heat and let sit with lid on until dish is finished.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp vegetable oil in a high-rimmed saucepan on medium-high heat.
  3. Add ground meat of choice. Break up/crumble and ensure all meat cooks fully & evenly.
  4. Once meat is finished cooking, stir in slaw mix. Cover and let steam for 2 mins.
  5. After steaming is done, stir in soy sauce, garlic powder and ground ginger. Ensure meat and slaw mixture is evenly flavoured.
  6. Plate over bed of rice, garnish as you wish.
  7. Enjoy!
