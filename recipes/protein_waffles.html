Protein Waffles

Description of Dish

A protein-rich, healthy way to start the morning. I love having these post-workout; they're filling, macro-friendly and most importantly super delicious! I am thankful for this video as I never would have thought to make these myself!

Ingredients Needed:

  • Two (2) servings protein powder of your choice (I use the Snickerdoodle flavour from PEScience)
  • One hundred and eighty four (184) grams of egg whites or eegg starters
  • Fourteen (14) grams of coconut flour
  • Four (4) grams of baking powder
  • One (1) cup of unsweetened apple sauce
  • A low-calorie syrup
  • Optional: 1/3 cup unsweetened vanilla almond milk
  • Optional: additional cinnamon powder (adjust to taste as needed)

Steps:

  1. Pre-heat waffle maker. Lightly brush with a neutral oil (vegetable works fine).
  2. Add your egg whites/starters and protein powder to a mixing bowl. Whisk to combine.
  3. Whisk in coconut flour, baking powder and unsweetened apple sauce.
    • - Optional: sprinkle in some cinnamon powder every few whisks.
  4. Scoop and pour batter into iron, ensuring to not over-fill or under-fill the iron.
    • - (It may take a few tries, but don't worry!
  5. Cook batter according to waffle maker settings.
  6. Using a wooden spatula/kitchen tool, take the finished waffle off the iron and onto a plate.
  7. Repeat until no batter remains.
  8. Plate separately and drizzle with syrup.
  9. Enjoy!
